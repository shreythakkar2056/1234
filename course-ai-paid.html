Your Journey to Success

A structured 8-week recorded program with clear weekly deliverables

Week 1

Orientation + Marketing Fundamentals

Marketing funnels, customer journey, ICP & persona, value propositions, STP, goals & KPIs.

  • Tools/Platforms: Slides, Worksheets, Google Docs/Sheets
  • Deliverable: Define 1 ICP, map funnel & KPIs for a chosen niche
  • Assessment: Short MCQ + worksheet review
Week 2

Web Basics: Landing Pages & Funnels

Domain/hosting basics, WordPress/Elementor setup, funnel types, lead forms, GTM/GA4 install.

  • Tools/Platforms: WordPress, Elementor, Google Tag Manager, GA4, Form plugins
  • Deliverable: Launch a 1-page lead-gen landing with GA4 tracking
  • Assessment: Auto-graded checklist + tracking validation
Week 3

Content & Copy Foundations

Hooks & angles, ad frameworks (AIDA/PAS/4C), content pillars, Canva basics, short-form video briefs.

  • Tools/Platforms: ChatGPT, Canva, Google Sheets (content calendar)
  • Deliverable: 5 ad copies + 5 social posts + 1 content calendar (2 weeks)
  • Assessment: Rubric on clarity, relevance, CTA
Week 4

Organic Social Starter

IG/FB/LinkedIn basics, growth levers, Reels basics, creator guidelines, UGC briefs.

  • Tools/Platforms: Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Meta Business Suite
  • Deliverable: Publish a 7-day posting plan + 2 reels scripts
  • Assessment: Auto-check calendar completeness
Week 5

Meta Ads Introduction

Objectives, campaign structure, pixel/events, target audiences, creative formats.

  • Tools/Platforms: Meta Ads Manager, Events Manager
  • Deliverable: Draft a lead-gen campaign plan (objective, audiences, budget, creatives)
  • Assessment: MCQ + campaign plan rubric
Week 6

Google Ads Introduction

Account structure, keyword research, match types, ad strength, extensions, conversion tracking.

  • Tools/Platforms: Google Ads, Keyword Planner, Search Console (overview)
  • Deliverable: Draft a search campaign with keywords, ads, extensions
  • Assessment: MCQ + keyword quality rubric
Week 7

SEO Basics

Topical research, on-page essentials, internal links, technical basics, link-earning fundamentals.

  • Tools/Platforms: Google Keyword Planner, Google Trends, Search Console (demo), Screaming Frog (intro)
  • Deliverable: Publish an SEO-optimised blog (outline + on-page checklist)
  • Assessment: On-page checklist score
Week 8

Analytics, Automation & Mini Capstone

GA4 reports, Looker dashboards, Email/WhatsApp intro automations, simple zaps.

  • Tools/Platforms: GA4, Looker Studio, Mailchimp/Zoho Campaigns, Interakt/WATI, Zapier/Make
  • Deliverable: Mini Capstone: 2-page plan + 1 dashboard mock + 1 ads brief
  • Assessment: Final review checklist

Outcomes

  • ✔Launch working funnels & campaigns
  • ✔Dashboard mock in Looker
  • ✔Mini capstone presentation
  • ✔Client‑ready playbooks & templates
  • ✔Foundations in analytics & automation
