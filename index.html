What is ConnectoGrowth?

+

ConnectoGrowth is a Digital Marketing Education & Coaching Institute founded by agency owners who have trained 500+ students while actively running client projects. We focus on practical, hands-on training with real-world projects and mentorship from industry experts.

How is ConnectoGrowth Diffrent from other institutes?

+

Unlike traditional institutes, we are industry first. You’ll work on live projects, real campaigns, and agency style learning not just theory. Plus, you get mentorship directly from practicing marketers.

What courses does ConnectoGrowth offer?

+

Currently, we offer two main programs:

  • 4-Month Advanced diploma in digital Marketing & Ai (ideal for beginners & job-seekers).
  • 11-Month Masters in Digital Marketing & Ai (for high-paying skills, agency-level projects, and leadership training).

Will I learn AI tools as well?

+

Yes. We integrate 36+ AI tools, automation platforms, and data-driven marketing strategies into every module.

Do you provide job placement?

+

We provide 100% Placement Assistance through resume building, mock interviews, career mentorship, and 700+ direct hiring connections with agencies & companies.

Entry-level roles start between ₹3 LPA to ₹6 LPA, while advanced learners who complete the 11-month program may secure ₹6 LPA to ₹10.5 LPA depending on skills & performance.

Do you help with freelancing or agency setup?

Yes. Apart from jobs, we also train students in freelancing, client pitching, and agency growth strategies.

No. Our programs are designed for students, freshers, and working professionals. You’ll start from basics and gradually move to advanced topics.

Do you offer EMI or installment options?
